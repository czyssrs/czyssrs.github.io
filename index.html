About me

I'm Zoey (Zhiyu) Chen, and I am a Research Scientist at Meta (Facebook) Reality Labs. Before that, I obtained my PhD in Computer Science at the University of California, Santa Barbara. I was advised by Prof. William Wang and Prof. Xifeng Yan.

I'm broadly interested in natural language processing and deep learning. Specifically, I have been working on building the AI models that can ground on knowledge sources and make interactions with the users, which covers the areas of dialogue and interaction systems, question answering, natural language generation, and knowledge reasoning.
